about smallorbit

From idea to release.
With you in the loop.

smallorbit builds Claude Code plugins that turn the AI development lifecycle into a complete loop — plan, execute, polish, ship — with a human checkpoint at every handoff.

Why these kits exist

AI-assisted development is fast, but speed without a loop is just chaos. Each kit in the smallorbit family handles one phase of the cycle, hands off cleanly to the next, and pauses where a human decision belongs: approving a spec, reviewing a stack of PRs, triaging polish findings, signing off on a release.

Together they make the loop legible. You always know which phase you're in, what the agent just did, and where you have to weigh in.

The kit ecosystem

  • speckit — plan it. Interview a feature into existence and file an epic plus child issues, all from a slash command.
  • swarmkit — execute it. Spawn isolated worktree agents in parallel, open a stacked PR per issue, merge bottom-up.
  • squadkit — orchestrate it. Multi-agent crews built from a small roles → squads → crews vocabulary, for execution or read-only discovery.
  • polishkit — polish it. Appraise craft, sweep dead code and stale artifacts, and polish cross-cutting issues in one pass.
  • flowkit — ship it. Branch, commit, PR, merge, cut releases, and tag — the full develop → main lifecycle behind one set of slash commands.
  • sessionkit — throughout. Hand off context, resume seamlessly, plan work in flight, and capture reusable patterns from every session.
  • vaultkit — capture it. Read, search, edit, and capture into an Obsidian vault, all via the Obsidian CLI.

How they compose

The development-lifecycle kits form a complete loop: /spec plans, /swarm executes, /appraise and /sweep polish, and /release ships. /handoff and /pickup sit underneath all of them so context survives between sessions.

You can adopt them piecemeal — most teams start with speckit and swarmkit, then layer flowkit on top once they want a proper release pipeline. The kits are designed to be useful on their own and stronger together.

The principle

We don't believe in autonomous AI that ships code at you while you watch. We believe in AI that does the legwork at every phase and stops for the decisions only you can make. That's what "with you in the loop" means — the loop is yours, the kits just keep it spinning.
